Smart Kitchen  Solution

Smart Recipe Assistant

Displayed on a large TV screen, this system helps kitchen staff organise their work. The system displays each dish colour-coded by type and prioritises dishes based on customer waiting time, as well as helping staff with ingredients and directions adaptable to various types of restaurants. It will also collate similar dishes, resulting in decreased preparation times, reducing the waiting time for the customer to a minimum.

Smart Waiter Assistant

Displayed on a screen on the wall near the pass-through window, the Smart Waiter Assistant helps serving staff speedily and correctly deliver dishes, preventing dishes being delivered to the wrong table and removing the need to manually tick orders. If there are mistakes, they will be detected quickly. Serving staff or managers can also use the screen or their own mobile devices to easily check the status of any dish, the remaining orders for each table or waiting time.
